FRAUD SCORING BREAKDOWN
================================================================================

Scoring Rules:
- AI image suspicion: +40 points
- Image reused online: +30 points
- Suspicious URL: +20 points
- Unverified merchant: +20 points
- Fraud case similarity: +20 points
- Security threat: +25 points
- Metadata anomaly: +15 points
- Image manipulation: +35 points

Decision Thresholds:
- 0-30: APPROVE refund
- 31-70: MANUAL REVIEW
- 71-100: REJECT refund
